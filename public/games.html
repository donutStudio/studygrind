How to play GRIND WARS: Ice monsters are attacking! Answer questions correctly to gain mana to use your set of 4 weapons. Each answer choice will equip a different weapon. Your mana constantly decreases over time, and the longer you play, the faster and faster it will go down. Being hit by enemies also lowers mana. If you run out of mana you lose.

CONTROLS: WASD to move. Space to jump. Number keys/scroll to switch weapons and answers. Left click to fire a weapon and select answer. Right click to shield (shield drains mana 2x faster while active).
